As Carrie

AP Art Studio Sustained Investigation on queerness, femininity and gender performance. Ended up being on queerness (transgender), gender dysphoria, and eventually landed me on cross-sex hormones, as the best things do. Very artist of me to ~discover myself~ through a series of artworks. Very embarrassing but I'm grateful for the friends we made along the way. Two of whom are pictured <3
